How to Play Mafia

Master the art of deception and deduction in this classic social deduction game

🎭 Game Roles

🔫
Mafia
Evil Team

Eliminate civilians at night and blend in during the day. Win when mafia members equal or outnumber civilians.

👥
Civilian
Good Team

Help identify and vote out mafia members during the day. Win when all mafia members are eliminated.

🔍
Detective
Good Team

Special civilian who can investigate one player each night to learn if they are mafia or civilian.

👩‍⚕️
Doctor
Good Team

Special civilian who can protect one player each night from mafia attacks.

🎮 How to Play

  1. 1

    Game Setup

    Players are secretly assigned roles. The game moderator announces who is mafia, civilian, detective, and doctor.

  2. 2

    Night Phase

    All players close their eyes. Mafia secretly choose a civilian to eliminate. Then detective investigates a player, and doctor protects someone.

  3. 3

    Day Phase

    Everyone opens their eyes. The moderator announces who was eliminated (if anyone). Players discuss and vote to eliminate someone.

  4. 4

    Voting

    Each player votes for who they think is mafia. The player with the most votes is eliminated and reveals their role.

  5. 5

    Win Conditions

    Civilians win if all mafia are eliminated. Mafia win when they equal or outnumber the remaining civilians.

💡 Pro Tips

As mafia, act like a civilian. Participate in discussions and help vote out other players to avoid suspicion.

Pay attention to voting patterns. Players who consistently vote with the mafia might be mafia themselves.

As detective, share your information carefully. Revealing your role too early makes you a mafia target.

Doctor, protect the detective or other key players. Sometimes protecting yourself is the right move.

Keep track of who defends whom. Mafia members often defend each other during voting.

Don't be afraid to change your mind. New information might reveal who the real mafia members are.

📋 Game Rules

✅

Players must keep their eyes closed during night phases unless instructed to act.

✅

No talking during night phase. All communication happens during day phase.

✅

Dead players cannot communicate with living players about game information.

✅

All players must participate in voting. Abstaining is not allowed.

✅

The moderator's decisions are final. Players must follow instructions.

✅

Have fun! It's just a game - don't take it too seriously or make personal attacks.
