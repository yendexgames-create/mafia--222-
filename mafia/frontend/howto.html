What is Mafia?

A social deduction game of deception — players receive hidden roles, perform secret night actions, then discuss and vote by day.

Start Playing

Quick

Secret roles, night actions, and daytime voting — bluff, detect, survive.

Roles

🔪
Mafia
Chooses a victim each night.
Mafia coordinate secretly and try to eliminate civilians.
🩺
Doctor
Protects one player at night.
Doctor can save a targeted player from elimination.
🕵️
Detective
Investigates one player at night.
Detective uncovers alignment clues (mafia or not).
👥
Civilian
Discusses and votes by day.
Use discussion and logic to find mafia.

Game Phases

🌙
Night
Secret actions: Mafia kill, Detective checks, Doctor saves.
🌞
Day
Open discussion and voting to eliminate suspects.

Winning Conditions

Civilians Win

All mafia are eliminated.

✔ Civilians prevail
Mafia Win

Mafia reach parity with civilians.

✖ Mafia dominates
